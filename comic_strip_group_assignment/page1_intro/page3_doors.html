Escape at Midnight – Panel 3: Doors

The hallway is narrow and quiet. The only sound is the distant ticking of the countdown.

At the end of the hall, there are two doors facing each other.

The door on the left is labeled in clear white letters: “EXIT.” It's the slightest bit ajar, and a soft light peeks out from the crack.

The door on the right is marked with a simple question mark: “?." Your hands itch to turn the knob.

A small speaker in the ceiling crackles to life:

“One door leads you out. The other leads you deeper. You have to choose before the timer hits zero.”

The student hesitates for a moment… then reaches for a handle.
