Escape at Midnight – Panel 2: The Clue

The studio looks the same, but something is very different now.

The student lets go of the locked door handle and walks back into the studio.

At the front desk, an open sketchbook is waiting. Every page is filled with rough perspective drawings of corridors and doors, as if someone has been designing a maze.

Beside the sketchbook, a tablet suddenly wakes up by itself. Large blue numbers appear on the screen:

00:09:59

The sound is silent, but the message is clear: there is a time limit.

Countdown

00:09:59

Wall note

“EVERY DOOR IS A DECISION.”

The blue light from the tablet spills across the desk and out into the room. It picks out a faint outline in the back wall.

Right between two rows of desks, a narrow door is now standing slightly open. It was not open a moment ago.

The student takes a breath, closes the sketchbook, and steps away from the desk. There is really only one thing left to do.
