Escape at Midnight – Panel 5: Deeper Mystery

You stayed to explore the mystery.

The student chooses the door with the “?” symbol.

As soon as the door opens, the countdown stops and freezes.

Countdown

00:00:03



Inside, the room is filled with screens of different sizes.

Each screen shows a different scene from the student’s life: old projects, unfinished ideas, designs that were never presented.

On the largest screen, new text appears:

“YOU DIDN’T ESCAPE. YOU UNLOCKED A NEW LEVEL.”

The door behind the student closes, but this room doesn’t feel like a prison.

It feels like the beginning of something unknown.

The student smiles, sits down at the central desk, and reaches for the keyboard.
