Escape at Midnight – Intro: Locked In

It’s almost midnight in the campus design studio. The computers hum quietly. Most of the lights are off.

A student finally finishes their last web design project of the semester. As they walk toward the exit, they hear a soft click.

The door handle doesn’t move. The studio is locked from the outside.

Suddenly, a monitor flickers on by itself. Slowly, a line of text types across the screen:

“IF YOU WANT TO LEAVE, FIND THE HIDDEN EXIT.”
