Escape at Midnight – Panel 4: Safe Exit

You escaped safely.

The student grabs the EXIT handle and pulls.

The door opens into a short, brightly lit corridor.

At the end of the corridor, there is an emergency exit with a green sign above it.

When the student steps outside, cool night air hits their face.

The countdown sound disappears.

The building looks normal from the outside, like nothing strange ever happened.


They are safe.

Still, as they walk home, the student can’t stop wondering:

“If I had opened the other door… what would I have found?”
